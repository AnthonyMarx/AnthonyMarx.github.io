About Me

Hello! My name is Anthony Marx. I am currently a sophomore at the University of Michigan's College of Literature, Sciences, and Arts majoring in Environment with minors in Business and Computer Science. I am striving to rethink the way we do business and organize society in a way that is sustainable and allows us to live in harmony with our environment. At college, I hope to gain the necessary tools and resources to come up with possible solutions for us to reduce our environmental impact through technology and how we approach business.

This year I have just completed an introductory computer science course offered at UofM. Within this course I was able to not only learn how to build several projects including a simulated elevator game, I was also given strong foundational skills on how to approach programming problems. The course was taught in C++ and I was able to work with a variety of group members throughout the semester. From this class and my others I have gained the ability to collaborate effectively despite the difficult virtual setting of the class.
